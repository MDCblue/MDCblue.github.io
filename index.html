Miami Dade College Programming Team

MDC Blue

  • MISSION:
    To foster a culture of learning within the Computer Science community of Miami Dade College, and to encourage members to seek out opportunities to expand their knowledge. In addition, students will be able to develop and fine-tune their skills.

  • PURPOSE:
    The main purpose of MDC Blue Programming Team is to provide for students interested in programming to come together to gain experience in developing real world applications as well as provide a place where students with this interest can network with employers of developers and other information system professionals.

  • MEMBERSHIP:
    This organization will be open to all students currently registered at Miami Dade College and taking at least one class at Hialeah Campus.

  • EXECUTIVE BOARD & OFFICERS:

    • President: Emmanuel Garcia

    • Vice-President: Carlos Abraham Hernandez

    • Secretary: Richard Toscano

    • Treasurer: Wendy Rodriguez
